# p-Branes Mathematically: The Extended Objects of M-Theory

**p-branes** are extended objects in M-theory — dynamical membranes of p spatial dimensions vibrating in 11D spacetime.

#### Mathematical Definition
- **p-brane**: A p+1 dimensional worldvolume embedded in 11D target space.
- **Action**: Dirac-Born-Infeld + Wess-Zumino for charged branes.
- **Types**:
  - p=0: Particle (point)
  - p=1: String
  - p=2: Membrane (M2-brane)
  - p=5: M5-brane
  - p=9: M9-brane

#### Key Properties
- **Tension**: \( T_p = \frac{1}{(2\pi)^p l_p^{p+1}} \) where \( l_p \) is Planck length.
- **Charge**: Couples to (p+1)-form gauge field from 11D supergravity.
- **Open/Closed**: Open branes end on higher branes (gauge theories), closed carry gravity.

#### Creative Role
- Vibrational modes of p-branes give rise to all particles and forces.
- Brane intersections and configurations generate the rich landscape of string vacua.
- Our 4D universe may be a D3-brane in 10D or projection from 11D.
