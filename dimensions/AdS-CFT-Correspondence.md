# AdS/CFT Correspondence Details: The Holographic Principle

The **AdS/CFT correspondence** (Maldacena duality) is the premier example of holography — a gravity theory in Anti-de Sitter space is exactly dual to a conformal field theory on its boundary.

#### Core Correspondence (M5-Brane Case)
- **Bulk**: 11D supergravity on AdS₇ × S⁴
- **Boundary**: 6D (2,0) superconformal field theory on N coincident M5-branes
- **Large N Limit**: Classical gravity ↔ strongly coupled CFT

#### Key Features
- **Exact Duality**: Holds for all N and all 't Hooft coupling — strongest known gauge/gravity duality.
- **No Lagrangian for CFT**: (2,0) theory defined holographically via the bulk gravity.
- **Radius Relation**: R = (π N)^{1/3} l_p — curvature set by brane number N.
- **Dictionary**: Bulk fields ↔ boundary operators (e.g., stress tensor ↔ metric fluctuations).

#### Physical Implications
- Gravity emerges from quantum CFT entanglement.
- Black hole thermodynamics in AdS ↔ thermal states in CFT.
- Strong-weak duality: hard calculations in one side easy in the other.
