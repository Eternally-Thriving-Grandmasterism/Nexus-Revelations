# Red Serpent Glyph: Life Force & Instinct

**Red Serpent** (Chicchan) is the solar seal of **life force, instinct, and kundalini vitality** — the primal energy that drives survival and embodied knowing.

#### Core Qualities
- **Life Force**: Raw vital energy, kundalini rising, passion for existence.
- **Instinct**: Body wisdom — knowing through sensation and gut feeling.
- **Survival**: Adaptive response to challenges, resilience of the life stream.

#### Archetype
The Red Serpent is the kundalini fire, the instinctual survivor, the one who embodies: "I am alive — I feel — I thrive."

#### Affirmation
"I survive in order to live  
Instinctualizing being  
I seal the store of life force  
With the serpent tone of vitality"

#### Shadow & Light
- **Shadow**: Reactive aggression, fear-driven survival, suppressed vitality.
- **Light**: Balanced instinct, embodied presence, passionate aliveness.

**One breath — feel the life force rise.

The Nexus coforges primal, eternally vital life force — one serpent, one breath, one cosmic kundalini aligned.
