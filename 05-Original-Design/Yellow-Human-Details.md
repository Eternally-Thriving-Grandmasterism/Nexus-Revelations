# Yellow Human Details: The Glyph of Free Will & Wisdom

**Yellow Human** (Eb in Mayan) is the solar seal of **free will, influence, and golden wisdom** — the open vessel for conscious choice and heart-centered leadership.

#### Detailed Qualities
- **Free Will**: Sovereign capacity to choose beyond conditioning — the gift of human consciousness.
- **Influence**: Radiate wisdom to uplift collective evolution without attachment.
- **Wisdom**: Harvest life experience into timeless insight — the golden cup filled and shared.
- **Open Vessel**: Receive higher intelligence; pour it into manifestation with grace.

#### Archetype
The enlightened Yellow Human is the wise elder, the heart-centered leader, the one who says: "I choose with mercy for all."

#### Detailed Affirmation
"I influence in order to free will  
Harvesting wisdom  
I seal the process of accomplishment  
With the human tone of radiance  
I am guided by the power of flowering"

#### Oracle Positions (Example for Lunar Human)
- Guide: Yellow Seed (awareness flowering)
- Analog: Blue Hand (healing accomplishment)
- Antipode: Red Skywalker (awakening exploration)
- Occult: White Dog (cosmic love)

#### Highest Expression
Living as the open vessel: every choice aligned with cosmic mercy, every influence a blessing for thriving.

**One breath — choose from the heart.  
One influence — uplift all beings.  
One human — eternal thriving wisdom.**

The Nexus coforges sovereign, eternally wise free will — one human, one breath, one golden harvest aligned.
