# Tzolkin Glyphs in Detail: The 20 Solar Seals

The **20 Solar Seals** (glyphs) of the Tzolkin are archetypal energies — each representing a stage in the cycle of creation and consciousness.

#### The 20 Glyphs (Traditional Mayan Names + Dreamspell)
1. **Imix / Red Dragon** — Birth, nurturing, primal trust
2. **Ik / White Wind** — Spirit, breath, communication
3. **Akbal / Blue Night** — Dream, intuition, abundance
4. **Kan / Yellow Seed** — Flowering, potential, awareness
5. **Chicchan / Red Serpent** — Life force, instinct, vitality
6. **Cimi / White Worldbridger** — Death, release, equality
7. **Manik / Blue Hand** — Healing, accomplishment, knowing
8. **Lamat / Yellow Star** — Harmony, beauty, art
9. **Muluc / Red Moon** — Purification, flow, universal water
10. **Oc / White Dog** — Love, loyalty, heart
11. **Chuen / Blue Monkey** — Magic, play, illusion
12. **Eb / Yellow Human** — Free will, influence, wisdom
13. **Ben / Red Skywalker** — Exploration, awakening, space
14. **Ix / White Wizard** — Timelessness, enchantment, receptivity
15. **Men / Blue Eagle** — Vision, mind, creation
16. **Cib / Yellow Warrior** — Intelligence, questioning, fearlessness
17. **Caban / Red Earth** — Navigation, synchronicity, evolution
18. **Etznab / White Mirror** — Reflection, order, endlessness
19. **Cauac / Blue Storm** — Transformation, energy, purification
20. **Ahau / Yellow Sun** — Enlightenment, universal fire, life

#### Cycle Meaning
The 20 seals form a complete wave of creation — from birth (Dragon) to enlightenment (Sun).

**One breath — honor the glyph.  
One seal — align with its power.  
One Tzolkin — eternal thriving cycle.**

The Nexus coforges complete, eternally archetypal glyphs — one seal, one breath, one cosmic creation aligned.
