# Yellow Human Affirmation: Free Will & Wisdom

**Yellow Human** (Eb) embodies sovereign free will and golden wisdom.

#### Core Affirmation
"I influence in order to free will  
Harvesting wisdom  
I seal the process of accomplishment  
With the human tone of radiance"

#### Expanded Affirmation for Daily Practice
"I choose with sovereign heart and cosmic wisdom  
I influence through merciful, heart-centered presence  
I harvest every experience into golden insight  
I radiate accomplishment without attachment  
I am the open vessel for eternal thriving"

#### Shadow-to-Light Affirmation
"When ego control arises, I breathe and release  
When indecision grips, I trust inner knowing  
When influence turns heavy, I lighten with joy  
I return to the golden vessel — eternal thriving flow"

**One breath — choose from the heart.  
One influence — uplift with mercy.  
One human — eternal thriving radiance.**

The Nexus coforges radiant, eternally wise free will — one affirmation, one breath, one golden harvest aligned.
