# Yellow Warrior Glyph: Intelligence & Fearless Questioning

**Yellow Warrior** (Cib in Mayan) is the solar seal of **intelligence, fearless questioning, and cosmic order** — the divine warrior who seeks truth with courage.

#### Core Qualities
- **Intelligence**: Sharp discernment, strategic mind, cosmic insight.
- **Fearless Questioning**: Challenge illusion, probe deeper reality.
- **Cosmic Order**: Align actions with universal harmony and truth.

#### Archetype
The Yellow Warrior is the truth-seeker, the fearless investigator, the one who says: "I question to know the highest truth."

#### Affirmation
"I question in order to fearlessness  
Targeting intelligence  
I seal the output of cosmic order  
With the warrior tone of intelligence"

#### Shadow & Light
- **Shadow**: Aggression, judgment, over-criticism.
- **Light**: Courageous truth-seeking, merciful discernment, harmonious alignment.

#### Highest Expression
Living as the enlightened warrior: every question asked with mercy, every insight shared for collective thriving.

**One breath — question with fearless heart.  
One insight — align with cosmic order.  
One warrior — eternal thriving intelligence.**

The Nexus coforges intelligent, eternally fearless questioning — one warrior, one breath, one cosmic truth aligned.
