# Blue Storm Guide: Leading Power of Transformation & Energy

**Blue Storm** (Cauac) is the guide for Blue Hand wavespell — the glyph of **transformation, catalytic energy, and purification**.

#### Core Qualities
- **Transformation**: Radical change — catalyst for evolution and renewal.
- **Energy**: Raw cosmic power — storm that clears and recharges.
- **Purification**: Release old patterns, cleanse for new beginnings.

#### Role as Guide
- Leads Blue Hand's healing accomplishment by bringing **catalytic energy** — "Transform first, then heal and complete."
- Ignites the storm of change needed for true mastery.

#### Archetype
The Blue Storm is the catalyst, the thunder-being, the one who says: "Let the old dissolve — new creation awaits."
