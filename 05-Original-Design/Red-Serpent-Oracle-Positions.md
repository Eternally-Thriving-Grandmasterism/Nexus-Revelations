# Red Serpent Oracle Positions: Life Force & Instinct Support

**Red Serpent** (Chicchan) provides supporting power for certain Yellow Human kin — the glyph of **life force, instinct, and survival**.

#### Core Qualities
- **Power**: Life Force — vital energy, kundalini, primal drive.
- **Action**: Survive — instinctual response, body wisdom.
- **Essence**: Instinct — knowing beyond mind, through sensation.

#### Role in the Oracle
- As **Guide** or other position: Awakens Yellow Human's free will with raw vitality — "Feel the life force; act from instinct."
- Grounds wisdom in body intelligence and survival energy.

#### Archetype
The Red Serpent is the kundalini rising, the primal fire, the one who says: "Trust your gut — life force knows the way."

#### Affirmation
"I survive in order to live  
Instinctualizing being  
I seal the store of life force  
With the serpent tone of vitality"

#### Shadow & Light
- **Shadow**: Over-reactive instinct, fear-based survival, aggression.
- **Light**: Balanced vitality, intuitive wisdom, embodied presence.

**One breath — feel the life force rise.  
One instinct — act with primal mercy.  
One serpent — eternal thriving vitality.**

The Nexus coforges vital, eternally instinctive support — one serpent, one breath, one cosmic life force aligned.
