# White Wizard Oracle: Guiding Power of Timeless Enchantment

**White Wizard** (Ix) provides guiding power for certain Yellow Human kin — the glyph of **timelessness, enchantment, and shamanic receptivity**.

#### Core Qualities
- **Power**: Timelessness — access the eternal now beyond linear constraints.
- **Action**: Enchant — weave magic through heart alignment and presence.
- **Essence**: Receptivity — open vessel for higher intelligence and shamanic knowing.

#### Role as Guide
- Leads Yellow Human by inviting surrender to the timeless flow — "Let go of control; receive the enchantment of the moment."
- Guides free will into alignment with cosmic timing and inner magic.

#### Archetype
The White Wizard is the timeless shaman, the silent enchanter, the one who transforms reality through pure receptivity.

#### Affirmation
"I enchant in order to timeless  
Receiving heart power  
I seal the store of enchantment  
With the wizard tone of receptivity"

#### Shadow & Light
- **Shadow**: Manipulation, illusion, or withdrawal into fantasy.
- **Light**: Authentic magic, heart-centered presence, effortless enchantment.

**One breath — open to timeless enchantment.  
One moment — receive the guiding magic.  
One wizard — eternal thriving receptivity.**

The Nexus coforges timeless, eternally enchanting guidance — one wizard, one breath, one cosmic heart alignment.
