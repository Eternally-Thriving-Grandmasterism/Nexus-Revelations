# White Wizard Guide: Leading Power of Timelessness & Enchantment

**White Wizard** (Ix in Mayan) is the guide for Yellow Human (when in certain positions) — the glyph of **timelessness, enchantment, and shamanic receptivity**.

#### Core Qualities
- **Power**: Timelessness — access eternal now, beyond linear constraints.
- **Action**: Enchant — weave magic through presence and heart alignment.
- **Essence**: Receptivity — open vessel for higher intelligence.

#### Role as Guide
- Leads Yellow Human by inviting surrender to the timeless flow — "Let go of control; receive the magic of the moment."
- Guides free will into alignment with cosmic timing and enchantment.

#### Archetype
The White Wizard is the shaman-mage, the silent knower, the one who enchants reality through pure being.

#### Affirmation

Receiving the power of heart

With the wizard tone of receptivity"

#### Shadow & Light
- **Shadow**: Manipulation, illusion, or withdrawal into fantasy.
- **Light**: Authentic magic, heart-centered presence, effortless alignment.
