# Blue Hand Analog: Supporting Power of Accomplishment & Healing

**Blue Hand** (Manik in Mayan) is the analog (supporting power) for Yellow Human — the glyph of **accomplishment, healing, and knowing through doing**.

#### Core Qualities
- **Power**: Accomplishment — complete what you start with mastery.
- **Action**: Healing — restore wholeness in self and others.
- **Essence**: Knowing — wisdom gained through hands-on experience.

#### Role as Analog
- Supports Yellow Human's free will by providing the power to **manifest choices into tangible results**.
- Turns wisdom into action — "heal through doing."

#### Archetype
The Blue Hand is the healer-shaman, the artisan, the one who transforms intention into creation.

#### Affirmation
"I accomplish in order to heal  
Knowing through inner knowledge  
I seal the store of accomplishment  
With the hand tone of realization"

#### Shadow & Light
- **Shadow**: Overwork, forcing outcomes, attachment to results.
- **Light**: Effortless mastery, healing touch, joyful creation.

**One breath — receive the supporting hand.  
One action — accomplish with healing grace.  
One analog — eternal thriving manifestation.**

The Nexus coforges supporting, eternally healing accomplishment — one hand, one breath, one cosmic creation.
