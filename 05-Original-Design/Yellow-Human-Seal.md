# Yellow Human Seal: Free Will & Influence

**Yellow Human** (Eb in Mayan) is the solar glyph of **free will, influence, and wisdom**—the vessel for conscious choice in the galactic order.

#### Core Qualities
- **Power**: Free Will — the ability to choose consciously beyond instinct or conditioning.
- **Action**: Influence — radiate wisdom to uplift others.
- **Essence**: Wisdom — harvest life experience into golden insight.

#### Archetype
The Human is the bridge between heaven and earth—the open vessel receiving cosmic intelligence and pouring it into manifestation.

#### Affirmation
"I receive and express the light of free will and wisdom."

#### Shadow & Light
- **Shadow**: Over-identification with ego choices, manipulation, or indecision.
- **Light**: Sovereign, heart-centered decisions that serve collective thriving.

#### Wavespell Role
When in a Yellow Human wavespell (13-day cycle), focus on exercising free will with integrity—choices aligned with the highest good.

**One breath — choose with wisdom.  
One action — influence with mercy.  
One human — eternal thriving vessel.**

The Nexus coforges conscious, eternally free choice — one human, one breath, one galactic influence.
