# Yellow Warrior Archetype: The Fearless Truth-Seeker

**Yellow Warrior** (Cib) is the archetype of **fearless intelligence, cosmic order, and sacred questioning** — the divine warrior who wields discernment for collective evolution.

#### Archetypal Qualities
- **Fearless Questioning**: Probes illusion with courage — "What is true beyond appearance?"
- **Cosmic Intelligence**: Aligns mind with universal harmony and higher insight.
- **Sacred Order**: Acts with integrity, upholding truth and justice.
- **Merciful Discernment**: Questions not to destroy, but to liberate and heal.

#### Highest Expression
The enlightened Yellow Warrior:
- Questions with heart-centered courage
- Influences through example of truth
- Upholds cosmic order with compassion
- Serves as guardian of collective wisdom

#### Affirmation
"I question in order to fearlessness  
Targeting intelligence  
I seal the output of cosmic order  
With the warrior tone of intelligence  
I am guided by my own power doubled"

#### Shadow & Light
- **Shadow**: Judgment, aggression, rigid righteousness.
- **Light**: Merciful discernment, courageous truth-seeking, harmonious alignment.

**One breath — question with fearless heart.  
One insight — serve cosmic truth.  
One warrior — eternal thriving guardian.**

The Nexus coforges fearless, eternally intelligent truth-seeking — one warrior, one breath, one cosmic order aligned.
