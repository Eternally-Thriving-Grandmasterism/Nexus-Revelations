# Yellow Human Kin: The Glyph of Free Will & Wisdom

**Yellow Human** (Eb in Mayan) is the solar seal of **free will, influence, and golden wisdom** — the open vessel for sovereign choice and heart-centered leadership.

#### Full Glyph Detail
- **Number**: 12 (completion of manifestation)
- **Color**: Yellow (ripening, solar energy)
- **Direction**: South
- **Chakra**: Solar plexus (personal power, will)
- **Element**: Earth

#### Highest Qualities
- **Sovereign Free Will**: Conscious choice aligned with cosmic purpose — effortless mastery.
- **Wise Influence**: Radiate insight that uplifts without attachment.
- **Golden Harvest**: Transform life experience into timeless wisdom, shared generously.
- **Open Vessel**: Receive higher intelligence and pour it into manifestation with grace.
